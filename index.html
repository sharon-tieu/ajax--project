Hiyao Miyazaki is a notorious Japanese animator who beautifully constructs immersive worlds in his storytelling. Through masterful animation and attention to detail, Studio Ghibli consistently crafts nostalgic and unique films that captivates audience members.

Each Studio Ghibli film demonstrates how humans should foster a respectful relationship with nature as each theme consists of the war between nature and industrialism. Are humans able to co–exist harmoniously with nature? How has human individualism and greed affect others around us and the environment?

"The creation of a single world comes from a huge number of fragments and chaos."

— Hiyao Miyazaki
